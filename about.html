About Us

Welcome to Sweden — your ultimate guide to exploring one of Scandinavia's most beautiful and culturally rich countries. We are a passionate team of travel enthusiasts, writers, and locals who have come together to share our love for Sweden with the world.

Our journey began in 2020 when a small group of friends realized that despite Sweden's incredible landscapes, vibrant cities, and unique cultural heritage, many travelers didn't know where to start when planning their Swedish adventure. We wanted to change that. We wanted to create a place where curious minds could come to learn, explore, and fall in love with Sweden the way we have.

From the cobblestone streets of Gamla Stan in Stockholm to the breathtaking fjords of the west coast, from the midnight sun of Lapland to the sandy beaches of Gotland, Sweden has something for everyone. Our mission is to bring all of that to you in one place, with honest, well-researched, and beautifully written content that inspires and informs.

We cover everything — city guides, nature trails, food and drink, history and culture, travel tips, seasonal events, and hidden gems that most tourists never find. Whether you are planning your first trip to Sweden or your tenth, whether you are interested in the famous ABBA Museum or a remote cabin in the wilderness, we have got you covered.

Our team is spread across Sweden, with contributors in Stockholm, Göteborg, Malmö, Uppsala, and beyond. Each of our writers brings a unique perspective and a deep personal connection to the places they write about. We believe that the best travel content comes from people who truly know and love the places they describe.

We are also committed to responsible and sustainable travel. Sweden is a country that takes its environmental responsibilities seriously, and so do we. We encourage our readers to travel thoughtfully, respect local communities, and leave the places they visit better than they found them.

Thank you for being here. We hope that through our content, Sweden becomes not just a destination on your bucket list, but a place that feels like a second home.
