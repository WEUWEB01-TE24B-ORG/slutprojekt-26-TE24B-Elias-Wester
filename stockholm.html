Stockholm

Sweden’s capital is spread across 14 islands, offering a unique mix of historic charm and modern design. Visitors can explore its scenic waterfronts, world-class museums, and the beautifully preserved old town, Gamla Stan.

Activities

In Stockholm, visitors can wander through the cobbled streets of Gamla Stan, explore world-class museums like the Vasa Museum, and enjoy scenic boat tours between the islands. The city also offers great shopping, waterfront dining, and peaceful green spaces perfect for a break from sightseeing.

Tips

Visit Gamla Stan early in the morning to enjoy its charm without crowds, and use public transport or ferries to explore the islands with ease. Be sure to pack layers, as the weather can change quickly even during summer.
