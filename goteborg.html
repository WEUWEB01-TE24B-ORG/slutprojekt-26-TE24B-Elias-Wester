Göteborg

Located on Sweden’s west coast, Gothenburg is known for its relaxed vibe, canals, and thriving food scene. The city is a gateway to stunning archipelagos and offers a blend of cultural attractions and maritime heritage.

Activities

In Gothenburg, you can stroll along charming canals, visit the lively Liseberg amusement park, and discover the city’s thriving food scene. A trip to the nearby archipelago is a must for nature lovers, offering beautiful coastal views and quiet island life.

Tips

Try fresh seafood at the famous Feskekôrka and take advantage of the city’s efficient tram system to get around. Don’t miss a boat tour to the nearby archipelago for a relaxing escape.
